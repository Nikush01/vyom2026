Game Rules & Info

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VYOM 2026. If you don't see your question here, feel free to contact us.

What is VYOM?
+
VYOM 2026 is the annual flagship fest of Rungta University, Bhilai. Themed around the Squid Game universe, it features 6 days of technical, cultural, sports, e-sports, fun, and creative events from March 23 to 28, 2026.
When and where will VYOM take place?
+
VYOM 2026 runs from 23rd to 28th March 2026 at the Rungta University campus, Bhilai, Chhattisgarh. Different events are spread across multiple venues on campus.
Who can participate in VYOM?
+
VYOM is open to all students of Rungta Institutions. Students from partner colleges may also participate in select open events. Check individual event pages for eligibility details.
How can I register for VYOM?
+
Click the Register Now button on this page or visit the Register page. Fill in your details, select your arenas of interest, and you'll receive a unique Player ID upon successful registration.
Can I form a team with students from different colleges?
+
For most team events, all members should be from the same institution. Some events allow cross-college teams — refer to the individual event rules in the brochure for specific details.
How can I purchase official merchandise?
+
Official VYOM 2026 merchandise is available on-site during the fest at the merchandise counter, and via the Merchandise page on this website. Limited quantities available.
